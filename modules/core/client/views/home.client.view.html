Welcome to Rock Point

We trust that you will find this web site to be useful as you learn about Rock Point Church for the first time or become involved with one of our ministries at a deeper level. We invite you to Worship Christ, Walk with Christ and Work for Christ with us!

New Here?

  • What do I wear?
  • Clothes. Seriously. We are much more concerned about what's going on inside of a person, than the clothes they wear on the outside. The pastors are usually wearing jeans.
  • Music
  • Our music is upbeat and our live band usually consists of guitars, drums, and keys. We try to sing songs that are deep in meaning, but easy to understand.
  • Children
  • We offer dynamic children services from birth to 5th grade during the Sunday morning gathering.
